OUR MISSION

We want to promote free tutoring opportunities to low-income and underserved communities' students and provide free flexible and remote volunteering opportunities.

Call Your Representative Today

There have been multiple attempts by the Trump Admistration to dismantle the United States Department of Education. Its budget and workforce has been significantly reduced. The Department of Education's main priority is to help reduce the inequality and funding gaps between all Americans. We urge you to call your rep's office today. FOR STUDENTS AND PARENTS:

We understand that tutoring, especially 1:1, can be expensive. With our resources, you can explore multiple organizations that helps you or your child.

FOR TUTORS :

Not everyone has the ability to help out on the weekends or after-school every day due to personal issues. With our resources, students/adults can find free, flexible, and remote volunteering opportunities that can be done at any time anywhere as long as you have an Internet connection, a working electronic device, and preferably a microphone!
